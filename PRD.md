# MeetMemo Product Requirements Document

**Version:** v2.3.1

## Overview

MeetMemo is a marketing and conversion-focused landing experience for the MeetMemo product. It provides a public-facing site with landing content, demo scheduling, and legal pages to support user acquisition and trust.

## Product Scope

- **Landing** – Main marketing landing page with value proposition, features, and calls-to-action.
- **Schedule demo** – Page or flow for users to book a product demo.
- **Legal pages** – Privacy policy, terms of service, and other required legal content.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js
- **UI:** React, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
- **Icons:** Lucide icons

## Current Success Criteria

- Landing page loads and renders correctly across target devices and browsers.
- Schedule-demo flow is functional and captures user intent.
- Legal pages are present, linked, and readable.
- Site meets agreed performance and accessibility baselines.
